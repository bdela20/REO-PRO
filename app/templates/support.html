Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Real Estate Office Pro?

Simply sign up for your free 30-day trial, complete your profile setup, and start exploring our features. Our onboarding guide will walk you through the basics.

Can I import my existing client data?

Yes! We support importing contacts from CSV files, Google Contacts, and most CRM systems. Contact support for assistance with large data migrations.

Is there mobile app available?

Yes, our mobile app is available for both iOS and Android. All your data syncs automatically between your mobile and web accounts.

How does MLS integration work?

We integrate with major MLS systems to provide real-time property data. Setup varies by region - our support team will help configure your MLS access.

What if I need to cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period, and we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee.

Do you offer team or brokerage pricing?

Yes! We have special pricing for teams of 5+ agents and comprehensive brokerage solutions. Contact our sales team for custom pricing.
